Blindbox Newsletter

Crystal Zhou, 2025

A typography-focused newsletter exploring blindbox culture, balancing visual storytelling and text to engage readers while educating them about the origins and history of blindboxes.

Technical Approach

Skills: Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, Typography, Layout Design

The newsletter was created using Adobe InDesign and Photoshop. Each page balances visual storytelling and informative content, employing grid systems, typographic hierarchy, and layout techniques to maximize readability and engagement.
